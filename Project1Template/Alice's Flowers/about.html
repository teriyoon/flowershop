About Us

Alice's Flowers was founded by Alice the Florist at Chicago, Illinois. Alice started working in a flower shop temporarily during late 2000s and was given the opportunity to open her own shop in 2014. Alice's Flowers is full of talented florists who loves nature and want to bring the beauty of nature to all. We provide fresh flowers and pay close attention to the aesthetic and details of the arrangements. We hope to bring happiness and joy through our flower arrangments.
